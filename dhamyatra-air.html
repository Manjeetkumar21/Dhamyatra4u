Char Dham Yatra by Air

Destination: Haridwar, Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, Badrinath

Duration: 4 nights, 5 days

Distance: From Delhi 550 KM

Experiences: Spiritual

Budget: Approx INR 1.5 lakh per person

START

DAY 1

DEHRADUN TO YAMUNOTRI

Your journey to Yamunotri commences and concludes at the government helipad situated on Sahastradhara Road in Dehradun. The flight schedules are predetermined and communicated beforehand. The helicopter service company's pilots and ground crew provide a comprehensive briefing to passengers, detailing the expectations for the trip, the flight itself, the off-ground activities, and other pertinent information. The flight takes off for Kharsali. Upon reaching Kharsali, you can embark on a palanquin ride to the Yamunotri temple, which is merely 5 km away. After paying your respects at the temple, you will return to Kharsali, where you will spend the night.

EXPLORE DESTINATION

DAY 2

KHARSALI TO GANGOTRI

The following morning, after your morning meal, you will embark on a helicopter journey to Harsil. Upon arrival, you will have the opportunity to visit the revered Gangotri temple, located approximately 25 kilometers away by road, a journey that typically takes around 45 minutes by taxi. Once you have concluded your prayers and devotions at the temple, you will make your way back to Harsil by road. In the evening, you can explore the charming town of Harsil at your leisure, before retiring for the night at your hotel accommodation.
