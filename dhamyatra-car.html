Char Dham Yatra by Air

The Char Dham Yatra is a sacred pilgrimage for Hindus, including the holy sites of Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. To legally operate vehicles on this important route, strict rules must be followed. Two key permits are needed: the Green Card and the Trip Card.

These permits are issued by the Transport Department or Regional Transport Offices. They confirm that commercial vehicles are allowed to travel on the Char Dham circuit. Getting these permits properly is crucial for ensuring pilgrims can have a safe and meaningful spiritual journey.

Green Card for Char Dham Yatra

A green card is a document that includes details about a vehicle's paperwork, such as its fitness certificate, road permit, tax payment receipt, insurance certificate, and pollution certificate. The Green Card permit is required for commercial vehicles traveling in the state of Uttarakhand. You can get the Green card after a physical inspection at any transport office in Uttarakhand. Green cards are issued once and are valid until either November 30th or for 30 days, whichever comes first. During the Green card's valid period, you can make an unlimited number of trips for the entire Char Dham Yatra. It is mandatory to renew the green card once it expires.

Documents & Fee Required for Green Card

Below are the documents necessary for acquiring the Green Card permit on the Char Dham route:

  • Registration certificate
  • Fitness certificate
  • Uttarakhand State Road Tax Deposit Certificate
  • Insurance certificate of Uttarakhand state
  • Pollution control certificate
  • Insurance certificate of the vehicle

Green Card Fee
The fee for the Green Card, according to the vehicle type, is provided below. The fee for the Green Card must be paid by the vehicle owner online at the time of application:

  1. Light motor vehicles: INR 600
  2. Medium motor vehicle: INR 800
  3. Heavy motor vehicle: INR 600
Trip Card for Char Dham Yatra

The trip card is an important part of the Char Dham Yatra. It serves as a record of the pilgrimage journey. It includes information like the travel date, bus license details, and passenger details. Importantly, each trip card is only valid for one round trip, so you need to get a new one for each additional journey. Before applying for the Trip Card, you must first register for the Char Dham Yatra on Yatra.gross, which can be easily done by registering at regis.uttarakhandtravellers.gov.in/ There is no fee to obtain the Trip Card and it can be gotten online easily. This careful process ensures proper documentation and following the rules throughout the pilgrimage, helping to provide a safe and well-organized travel experience for all pilgrims.

Documents Required for Trip Card

Below are the documents necessary for acquiring the Trip Card permit on the Char Dham route:

  • Vehicle pass
  • Driving License
  • Blank pie-chart on driver's license
  • Uttarakhand state permit
  • Tax payment certificate of Uttarakhand state
  • Passengers list traveling in the vehicle in the prescribed format
How to Apply Green Card & Trip Card for Char Dham Yatra

To apply for the Green Card and Trip Card for the Char Dham Yatra, visit the official website of the Transport Department at www.greencard.uk.gov.in. The Green Card can be obtained through an online application from any Regional Transport Office (RTO) or Sub-Regional Transport Office (SRTO) within Uttarakhand. After a physical inspection by the road inspection team, the Green Card can be received from any Transport office in Uttarakhand. Trip Cards for all types of vehicles are issued exclusively online.

Important Note: Private vehicles do not need to get a Green Card or Trip Card for the Char Dham Yatra. However, it is mandatory for even private vehicles to complete Char Dham registration if anyone is traveling in that vehicle. This registration helps follow the rules and allows families going on the pilgrimage to have a smooth journey.
